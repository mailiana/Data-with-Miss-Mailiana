ABOUT MISS MAILIANA

Miss Mailiana is a self-taught Data Analyst and Tech Educator on a mission to help people learn, earn, and grow in the tech space — no matter their background.

With over 2 years of experience in teaching data analytics and strategy, she has guided hundreds of learners through practical tools like Excel, Power BI, SQL, and Python. Her approach simplifies complex topics and turns beginners into confident data thinkers — many of whom have gone on to secure freelance gigs, land full-time roles, or generate income through affiliate opportunities.

From leading the 4Tool4 Data Course and personalized one-on-one coaching to building viral learning communities like the 100 Days of Data Challenge, her programs are known for clarity, community, and real-world impact.

As one student shared: "I joined with no background in data, and now I’ve built a strong portfolio, landed freelance projects, and gained the confidence to speak in stakeholder meetings.” Miss Mailiana believes tech should be accessible and actionable. Her platform isn’t just about skills — it’s about building strategy, storytelling, and structure around your growth.

Whether you’re just starting, pivoting careers, or looking to upgrade your decision-making with data — she’s here to guide you with support that gets results.
